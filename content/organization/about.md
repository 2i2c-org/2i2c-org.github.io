2i2c stands for **the International Interactive Computing Collaboration**.
We are a non-profit, fiscally-sponsored project of [Code for Science and Society](https://codeforscience.org), a US 501(c)(3) public charity.
This page is a summary of our team structure, see [our team compass](https://compass.2i2c.org/organization/structure) for more information about our organizational structure and roles.

## Roles we have in open source projects

In addition to our 2i2c roles, we hold a number of leadership and maintainer roles across the open source projects we build upon. These links show the membership of a few open source projects we work with deeply:

- **[JupyterHub team](https://compass.hub.jupyter.org/team/)** - tools for orchestrating and managing multi-user interactive computing environments for research and education
- **[Jupyter Book team](https://jupyterbook.org/community/team/)** - tools for building and sharing interactive, publication-quality scientific documents
- **[Jupyter Executive Council](https://jupyter.org/about#executive-council-members)** - overall governance and strategic direction of Project Jupyter
- **[Jupyter Distinguished Contributors](https://jupyter.org/about#distinguished-contributors)** - recognized for exceptional and sustained impact on the Jupyter community
