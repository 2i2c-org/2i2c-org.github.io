The [University of Toronto](https://www.utoronto.ca/) is one of 2i2c's longest-running community partnerships. 2i2c has operated a [university-wide JupyterHub](https://act.utoronto.ca/jupyterhub-support/) for UofT since 2021. It supports thousands of educators and learners across many departments and is one of our largest educational hub deployments.

UofT's large-scale usage has been a major driver of improvements to 2i2c's core infrastructure, helping us improve our operational practices around scalability, storage monitoring, and cost sustainability.
