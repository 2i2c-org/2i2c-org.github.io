# How we are funded

To sustain and grow our operations, 2i2c receives funding from the following sources:

- **Membership** - Communities join 2i2c's network to collaborate with our team and [peer organizations](../../members/index.md), pool resources toward shared open source needs, and help drive the direction of the tools that research depends on. It includes managed open infrastructure via our [community hub platform](../../platform/_index.md). See [our membership page](/membership) for more information.
- **Co-funded development projects** - Members and funders invest in specific development work that addresses shared infrastructure challenges on [our roadmap](https://2i2c.org/roadmap/), with improvements flowing back upstream to the open source communities we all rely on.
- **Core organizational support** - We raise philanthropic funding to cover core organizational costs. Our long-term goal is to fund operations entirely through the membership and co-development flywheel, and we're actively working toward that.
