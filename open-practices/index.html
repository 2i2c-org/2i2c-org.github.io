We believe that open science requires open practices - here are a few ways we lean into open practices and support open communities at 2i2c. See our open source strategy for more details about the practices we follow.

Support open technology with our service

  • Build our services with open technology and commit to licensing practices that prevent negative outcomes.
  • Give communities the Right to Replicate their infrastructure without 2i2c.
  • See a description of our platform

Co-create our impact with other communities

  • Take an upstream first approach and provide both Directed and Foundational open source contributions that we own as a team.
  • Acknowledge the contributions that our collaborators and funders have made in our work.
  • See our collaborators and funders

Do our work openly and transparently

  • Be transparent about organizations that support and fund us.
  • Show our work and priorities by communicating in-progress work on our blog, even if it’s in an imperfect state.
  • See our progress on our blog
